Initial Question Probe
I created an open-ended poll on my Instagram page to encourage people to share their stories with me.

Poll
“Tell me about a recent negative experience you had at a hospital.”

Purpose
Find real stories Ascertain pain points Narrow down hospitals

Speaking with Sadhna
Hospital: Changi General Hospital (CGH)
Reason: Broken Arm
No. of times visited: 3

Counter Staff
“..confusing even when the counter staff tells u where a room is located yet u can’t find ur way..”

Busy
“Especially when hospitals are packed and the staffs themselves look busy”

Overcome Shyness
“I resorted to asking them.. I was quite desperate.. Almost late for my xray appointment..”

Multiple Buildings
“I got confused at first.. Between the old and new buildings..”

Text Message Touchpoint
“The location.. Via text.. And on google maps.. Don’t show which building..”

Approaching Multiple Staff
“Eventually I approached another two ladies.. And they told me I was in the wrong building”

Time Wasted
“I even sat at the wrong building for a while.. Thinking I found the right place”

“Only after 10 minutes I realised I was at the wrong place”

Queue No. Touchpoint
“My queue number wasn’t similar to the rest.. Even (though) the facility name was similar”

Conclusion
There’s something interesting to explore for hospitals with multiple buildings—what is stopping the management from differentiating the buildings across touchpoints? How can we easily form that differentiation for visitors to recognise from the get-go? Can we utilise this pre-appointment messaging system to our advantage?
