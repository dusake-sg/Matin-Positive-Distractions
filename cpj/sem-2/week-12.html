A Good Week

Overall an enjoyable 3 days of showcase that was really helpful to practice the presentation for Viva as repeatedly explaining the project slowly revealed missing links, emphasised otherwise overlooked points and generally streamlined the oral pitch but quite a margin.

An example of an overlooked point was what inspired the project. I found myself pointing towards a small text cutout that had my reader and the main definition of ‚ÄòPositive Distraction‚Äô.
